Endpoint & Client Infrastructure Consulting

Clarity in Your End-User Computing Environment

Gigatech Solutions works with IT organizations in government, healthcare, and regulated industries to design, assess, and modernize their endpoint environments — providing the architecture and documentation your team needs before costly projects begin.

Request a Consultation View Our Services

Who We Help

Built for IT Organizations with Real Operational Stakes

We work with organizations where endpoint reliability, governance, and security are not optional — they are operational requirements.

IT Directors and endpoint teams managing 200 to 1,500 devices who need objective architecture guidance before committing to a platform or project direction.

Government agencies and public-sector IT organizations subject to compliance requirements, procurement constraints, and public accountability standards.

Healthcare and regulated-industry organizations where endpoint security controls, patch management, and policy governance carry direct compliance implications.

Teams preparing for major Windows lifecycle transitions, MDM migrations, or device refresh programs who need architecture and planning support before work begins.

Organizations evaluating MDM platforms — including Intune, Jamf, and similar solutions — and needing independent technical guidance to inform a platform decision.

IT leaders preparing for infrastructure audits, internal reviews, or leadership transitions who need a credible current-state assessment of their endpoint environment.

What We Do

Senior Consulting Across the Endpoint Lifecycle

Our work focuses on the architecture, standards, and governance layer of endpoint management — the decisions that determine whether your environment is sustainable over the long term.

Architecture & Assessment

We review your current endpoint environment and produce a documented picture of where you are, where the gaps are, and what a better-designed future state looks like.

MDM & Device Management

Whether you're deploying, migrating, or re-architecting your MDM environment, we provide technical consulting grounded in real-world implementation experience.

Endpoint Modernization

We help organizations plan and structure endpoint modernization initiatives — Windows lifecycle transitions, co-management migrations, and configuration standardization projects.

Fractional Endpoint Architect

For organizations that need ongoing senior architecture guidance, we offer a retainer-based advisory model — experienced input without the cost of a full-time hire.

Ready to Get Clarity on Your Endpoint Environment?

Most engagements begin with a short, informal conversation about where things stand. No obligation, no sales pitch — just a direct discussion about your situation.
