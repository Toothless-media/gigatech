About Gigatech Solutions

A senior consulting practice focused on endpoint architecture, device management, and end-user computing infrastructure — built on real-world enterprise IT experience.

The Practice

Senior Expertise in Endpoint Architecture and Client Infrastructure

Gigatech Solutions works with IT organizations that manage endpoint environments at scale — typically 200 to 1,500 devices — in sectors where reliability, security, and governance are operational requirements, not optional considerations.

We focus on the architecture and standards layer of endpoint management: the decisions that determine whether your environment is maintainable, auditable, and resilient over time. This is the work that happens before a project starts, not during it — and it's where most organizations have the largest unaddressed gaps.

Our clients include government agencies, healthcare organizations, educational institutions, and regulated private-sector organizations. What they share is a need for objective, senior guidance that is grounded in how enterprise IT environments actually function — not in vendor sales materials or framework checklists.

We are not a managed service provider, a helpdesk operation, or a web agency. We do one thing: senior consulting on endpoint environments. That focus means our engagements are thorough, our recommendations are specific, and our clients aren't competing for attention with other service lines.

Who You'll Work With

Andrew Benton, Founder & Principal Consultant

Gigatech Solutions is led by Andrew Benton, an engineering leader with eight years of experience as a senior engineer and DevOps manager in healthcare and public transit — sectors where infrastructure failures carry direct operational and compliance consequences.

His background spans the layers that endpoint environments depend on: automation and deployment pipelines, system architecture, security practices, and hands-on device management with platforms including Microsoft Intune and NinjaOne. That breadth is deliberate. Endpoint environments don't exist in isolation, and sound endpoint architecture requires understanding the identity, networking, and operational systems it connects to.

Before moving into engineering leadership, Andrew spent six years in technical customer-facing roles, coaching and supporting users directly. That experience shapes how Gigatech works today: findings are explained in terms your team and your leadership can act on — not delivered as jargon and left for you to decode.

Gigatech Solutions is a principal-led practice. The person you speak with in the first conversation is the person who does the work — there is no handoff to a junior team after the engagement is signed.

How We Operate

Our Operating Principles

These are not marketing language. They describe how we actually approach engagements, and what you should hold us to.

Architecture Before Configuration

Good endpoint management begins with sound architectural decisions — structure, standards, and governance design — before addressing configuration specifics. Starting in the wrong place produces environments that work initially and accumulate debt over time.

Documentation as a Foundation

An endpoint environment should be fully documented: its design rationale, its configuration standards, and its operational procedures. Without this, institutional knowledge lives in individuals rather than the organization, and every staff transition is a knowledge loss event.

Realistic Roadmaps

Improvement roadmaps are only useful if they account for your team's capacity, your budget cycle, and your actual operational constraints. We don't produce aspirational lists of everything that could theoretically be better. We produce plans you can act on.

Independent Perspective

Organizations benefit from outside review, particularly before major platform decisions or significant infrastructure changes. We bring a perspective that isn't shaped by internal politics, vendor relationships, or the assumptions that form when a team has been working in the same environment for years.
