Services

What We Offer

Our engagements focus on architecture, standards, and decision support — not implementation labor. We help your organization move forward with a clear picture of where it stands and a credible plan for where it's going.

Primary Engagement

Our Flagship Service

The assessment is designed for organizations that need a structured, documented review of their endpoint environment before making significant decisions.

Flagship Engagement

Endpoint Architecture & Readiness Assessment

A structured technical assessment of your organization's endpoint environment, device management practices, and governance posture — delivered as a clear, written report with prioritized recommendations.

What's Included

  • Initial stakeholder discovery sessions with IT leadership and endpoint team
  • Review of existing documentation, standards, and operational policies
  • Technical review of MDM configuration, enrollment architecture, and device lifecycle practices
  • Evaluation of imaging or deployment workflows and current-state automation
  • Assessment of endpoint security controls and patch management processes
  • Standards and governance gap analysis against appropriate frameworks and vendor guidance
  • Written findings report with documented current state and identified gaps
  • Prioritized improvement roadmap with specific, actionable recommendations

Who This Is For

This engagement suits organizations that have accumulated technical debt in their endpoint environment, are approaching a major project or platform decision, or need an independent review before committing resources. It is also valuable for teams that have recently undergone IT leadership changes and need a credible baseline of their current state.

What You Receive

A written assessment report suitable for internal review, leadership briefings, or vendor negotiations. The roadmap is written for your team — not dependent on our continued involvement to execute.

Typical Duration 3 – 4 weeks
Format Fixed-scope consulting engagement
Primary Deliverable Written assessment report & improvement roadmap
Engagement Start By mutual agreement after scoping call

Additional Services

Other Ways We Work With Organizations

Depending on your situation, these engagements may be better suited to your current needs than a formal assessment.

Service 01

Endpoint & MDM Architecture Consulting

For organizations that have a clear direction but need solid architectural guidance before or during implementation. We consult on MDM platform selection, enrollment architecture, policy framework design, configuration standards, and integration considerations with identity, security, and infrastructure systems.

This is not implementation labor. It is the upstream architecture work that determines whether your implementation is built on a sound foundation.

Scope and duration determined per engagement.

Service 02

Endpoint Modernization Projects

We work alongside internal teams on structured modernization initiatives: Windows lifecycle transitions, co-management or cloud-attach migrations, hardware refresh program planning, and configuration baseline standardization.

Our role is architectural oversight, documentation, and decision support — not to displace your team. We help ensure the project is structured correctly from the start, and that the work your team does builds toward a defensible and sustainable architecture.

Scope varies by project complexity.

Service 03

Fractional Endpoint Architect

For organizations that need ongoing, senior-level endpoint architecture guidance without the cost or overhead of a full-time hire, we offer a retainer-based advisory arrangement.

This typically covers architecture reviews of proposed changes, platform and tooling decisions, escalation support for complex technical questions, and longer-range planning input. Engagements are scoped individually based on the level of involvement required.

Retainer-based; contact us to discuss scope and fit.
