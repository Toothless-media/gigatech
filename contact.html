What to Expect

We respond to all inquiries within two business days. Initial conversations are informal — focused on understanding your situation — and carry no commitment or obligation.

If your inquiry needs more context before we can give a useful response, we'll ask a few follow-up questions rather than send a generic reply.

How Engagements Typically Begin

Most engagements start with a short conversation — a call or an exchange of emails — about your environment and what you're trying to accomplish. From there, if there's a clear fit, we discuss scope and timing.

We don't require an RFP or a formal procurement process to have an initial conversation, though we're comfortable working within those frameworks if your organization requires them.

Confidentiality All communications are treated as confidential. We do not share inquiry information, organizational details, or conversation content with third parties. Reaching out does not constitute a commitment to engage, and there is no obligation on your part at any stage of a preliminary conversation.
